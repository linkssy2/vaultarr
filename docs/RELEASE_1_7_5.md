# Vaultarr 1.7.5 — Calm Scan Drawer

The permanent **Scan Museum** pill now behaves like a quiet drawer instead of a resizing utility button.

## Changes

- The 44 px scan header stays anchored in the sidebar.
- Scan status unfolds beneath the header using a slower Vaultarr easing curve.
- Stage, percentage, progress, and current-game details reveal after expansion begins.
- Completion remains visible briefly, then details fade before the drawer folds closed.
- The scan icon rotates slowly while active.
- Reduced-motion preferences remain supported.

No changes were made to the scan engine, floating cards, card tilt, expansion, Focus Mode, sidebar navigation, Universal Search, manuals, or Acquisition Assistant.
