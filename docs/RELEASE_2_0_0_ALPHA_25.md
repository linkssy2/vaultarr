# Vaultarr 2.0.0 Alpha 25 — Living Milestone Meter

## Progress calculation

Milestone Progress is calculated from the sum of capped progress across every achievement divided by the sum of all achievement targets. This allows locked achievements to contribute gradually before they are fully earned.

## Visual treatment

- Breathing Vault-blue glow
- Liquid-like internal energy movement
- Moving highlight within the completed ring
- Traveling energy bead
- Reduced-motion fallback with static glow

No other Milestones content or protected interaction system was changed.
