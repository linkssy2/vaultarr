# Vaultarr 1.6.1 — The Museum Experience

Vaultarr 1.6.1 simplifies the standard experience around one principle: users manage their museum, not Vaultarr's internal systems.

## Highlights
- Library is presented as **Museum**.
- Curator is presented as **Activity** while the existing engine continues underneath.
- Home is redesigned as a museum lobby with the Blue V Orbital Core, collection completeness, recent exhibits, activity, and milestones.
- Milestones and Time Capsule remain visible in Standard Mode.
- Technical collection and preservation tools remain available in Advanced Mode.
- `/museum` and `/activity` aliases are added while `/library` and `/curator` remain compatible.
- `docs/UX_BASELINE.md` protects the stable 1.5.8 interaction systems.

## Stability
This is a presentation and information-architecture update. It does not rewrite the Floating Card Engine, Focus Mode, Universal Search, smooth navigation, manuals, or Curator job behavior.
